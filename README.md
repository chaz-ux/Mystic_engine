## 🔮 Casting (Usage)

Ensure your webcam is not being intercepted by another dimension (or app), stand in a well-lit room, and execute the core engine:

## 📜 Arcane Architecture (For Sorcerers & Devs)

Behind the illusions lies a robust framework of temporal and spatial mathematics:

* **Eldritch Geometry:** Custom 3D-to-2D projection math allowing 2D elements to rotate as if they exist in 3D space, alongside dynamic cubic Bezier curve generation for organic magic whips.
* **Particle Physics System:** A bespoke engine for calculating gravity, drag, velocity, and the flickering lifespan of thousands of individual portal sparks.
* **Additive Compositing:** Spells are drawn into a pure black void, Gaussian-blurred to simulate radiant bloom, and mathematically added back into the video feed to create a true, photorealistic "holographic" lighting effect.
